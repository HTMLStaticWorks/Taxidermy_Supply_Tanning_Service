The Story

Heritage & Craftsmanship

Three Generations Of Excellence

What started as a small local tanning service in 1995 has grown into a global leader in taxidermy supplies. Our founder, Arthur Vance, believed that the secret to a perfect mount lay in the chemistry of preservation.

Today, we continue that legacy by developing proprietary formulas and precision tools that allow artists to capture the raw beauty of the natural world with unprecedented accuracy.
